Geospatial Analysis of Denver Crime Data around RTD Bus Stops

This project takes Denver crime records for the past five years along with public transit stop data to explore connections between the two. Each reported crime is assigned to its nearest stop, with bubble size representing total crime count and color indicating the proportion of violent crimes. Stops with more than 33% violent crimes are outlined in red. This approach allows quick comparison of safer and more dangerous stops, though it may over- or under-represent crime at isolated or clustered stops and near notable locations like airports, police stations, or courthouses.
